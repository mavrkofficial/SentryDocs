# Bundle Sell

Bundle Sell lets the owner sell tokens held across bundle wallets.

## Sell All (Across Bundle Wallets)

1. Open Sentry Menu -> Deployer -> Bundle Sell
2. Tap Sell All
3. Paste the token address

The bot sells the token across all bundle wallets and posts a report.

## Partial Sell

1. Open Sentry Menu -> Deployer -> Bundle Sell
2. Tap Partial Sell
3. Paste the token address
4. Follow the prompts to choose wallets and amounts

---

Bundle Sell is owner-only and intended for launch management.
