# Viewing Positions

The Positions button shows active positions, performance, and quick actions.

## How to View Positions (Menu Flow)

1. Open Sentry Menu -> Positions
2. Choose one of the filters:
   - My Positions
   - Other Traders
   - Group Positions

## What You'll See

- Token name, symbol, and address
- Entry price and current price
- PnL and ROI
- Position size and invested amount
- DCA history (if applicable)

## Quick Actions

Positions include buttons for:

- Take Profit
- Sell

## Best Practices

1. Review Positions before making exits
2. Use filters to inspect other traders' calls
3. Track DCA history when averaging down

## Common Questions

### Why don't I see a position?
- It may have already been sold
- You selected a filter that excludes it

---

Use Positions regularly to stay on top of performance and manage exits.
