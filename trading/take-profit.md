# Take Profit (Partial Selling)

The Take Profit button sells a percentage of a position so you can lock gains while keeping exposure.

## How to Take Profit (Menu Flow)

1. Open Sentry Menu -> Take Profit
2. Choose a position from the list
3. Select a percentage to sell

## Percentage Options

Available options include:

- 10 percent
- 25 percent
- 50 percent
- 75 percent
- 100 percent (full exit)

## What Happens After a Take Profit

- Position size is reduced
- Entry price is recalculated if DCA was used
- PnL updates automatically
- Profits distribute if the sell is profitable

## When to Use Take Profit

Use it when:

- You want to lock profits
- You want a gradual exit
- You want to reduce risk

Use Sell when you want a full exit.

## Best Practices

1. Define targets before entering
2. Scale out in steps
3. Review results in Positions

---

Take Profit gives you flexible exits without fully closing the position.
