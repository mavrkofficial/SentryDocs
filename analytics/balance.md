# Balance and Portfolio

The Balance button gives a quick snapshot of your Group Wallet and portfolio value.

## How to View Balance

1. Open Sentry Menu -> Balance
2. Review SOL balance, USD value, and portfolio totals

## What You'll See

- Group Wallet SOL balance
- Approximate USD value
- Portfolio value including positions

## Tips

1. Check Balance before calls and DCAs
2. Keep a SOL buffer for gas fees

---

Balance is the fastest way to confirm funding before trading.
