# Setting Up Payout Wallets

Payout wallets are your personal Solana addresses where profits and distributions are sent.

## What is a Payout Wallet?

A payout wallet:

- Receives profit distributions
- Receives group pool payouts
- Is required to receive any earnings

## Who Can Set a Payout Wallet?

- Traders and members
- Anyone who sets a payout wallet is auto-added as a trader

## Setting Your Payout Wallet (Menu Flow)

1. Open Settings -> Payouts
2. Tap Set Payout Wallet
3. Paste your Solana wallet address when prompted

## Viewing All Payout Wallets

1. Open Settings -> Payouts
2. Tap Payouts

You will see the payout list and associated addresses.

## Wallet Address Format

- Base58 Solana address (32 to 44 characters)
- Example: `7xKXtg2CW87d97TXJSDpbD5jBkheTqA83TZRuJosgAsU`

## Changing Your Payout Wallet

Repeat Set Payout Wallet with your new address. The old address is replaced.

## Why You Need a Payout Wallet

Without one:

- You will not receive distributions
- You can still trade, but payouts go to others

## Best Practices

1. Use a secure wallet (Phantom, Solflare)
2. Double-check the address before submitting
3. Avoid exchange deposit addresses

---

Always set your payout wallet before trading so you receive distributions.
